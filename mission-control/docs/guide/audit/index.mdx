# Audit & Compliance

Mission Control provides audit-ready infrastructure reporting by scraping identity data, infrastructure state, and access logs from your cloud providers, identity systems, and databases into a unified catalog.

<AuditDiagram className="mx-auto" />

## What Mission Control Audits

- **Identity & Access** — Who has access to what, via Entra ID, AWS IAM, Kubernetes RBAC, and database roles
- **Access Logs** — Who actually accessed resources, when, and whether they used MFA
- **Infrastructure Inventory** — Complete catalog of resources with health status and change history
- **Backups** — Backup health, location, retention, and restore capability
- **Configuration Changes** — Immutable audit trail of every config change via diffs, CloudTrail, and Kubernetes events

## Compliance Frameworks

Access logs and identity data provide the audit trail required for:

- **SOC 2** — Access controls and monitoring evidence
- **HIPAA** — Track access to systems containing PHI
- **PCI-DSS** — Monitor access to cardholder data environments
- **ISO 27001** — Evidence for access management controls
